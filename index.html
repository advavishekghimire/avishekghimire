About Me

Dedicated to achieving the best outcomes for my clients

Professional Background

I am a dedicated litigation lawyer based in Biratnagar, Nepal. Practicing civil, criminal, corporate, and commercial disputes. I represent individuals, businesses, and organizations before various courts and legal bodies across Nepal, consistently achieving strong and favorable outcomes.

My practice includes criminal, civil, and corporate litigation. I adopt a strategic, client-focused approach, ensuring that every client receives clear guidance, personalized attention, and effective legal representation.

I am licensed to practice law in Nepal and regularly appear before District Courts, High Courts, and quasi-judicial authorities.
