Professional Background

I am a dedicated litigation lawyer based in Biratnagar, Nepal, with over 5+ years of experience handling civil, corporate, and commercial disputes. I have represented individuals, businesses, and organizations before various courts and legal bodies across Nepal, consistently achieving strong and favorable results.

My practice includes business and corporate litigation, contract disputes, intellectual property matters, and professional liability defense. I believe in a strategic and client-focused approach, ensuring that every client receives clear guidance, personalized attention, and effective legal representation.

I am licensed to practice law in Nepal and regularly appear before District Courts, High Courts, and quasi-judicial authorities, along with involvement in arbitration and mediation proceedings.
